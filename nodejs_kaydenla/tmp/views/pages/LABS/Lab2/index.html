COT Location

Attached is the location of the College of Technology- main Campus. This information is useful to COT students, as well as those looking into COT. Having an embedded map of the actual location is extremely useful to users.

 Firstly, this lets users know exactly where the COT building is. User’s will not be required to access external sites to get to the building. They will not be required to trust that external websites, such as Google Maps / Apple Maps, have correctly labeled the location of the COT building.
 Secondly, this reduces the click time for user-interaction. Webpages need to be fast and efficient. Every little inefficacy in a webpage detracts users substantially. Having an interactive map on the website itself makes users more inclined to stay on the website.
